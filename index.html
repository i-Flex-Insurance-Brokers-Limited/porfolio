About I-Flex Insurance Brokers

A Zambian-owned company with a commitment to excellence in insurance broking services since 2011

I-Flex Insurance Brokers Limited is a Zambian-owned company first incorporated on 21st November 2011. Since then, we have undergone tremendous transformation in quality service provision and organizational structure.

Our primary aim is to provide professional general insurance broking services on the Zambian market. We are established to provide quality insurance intermediary services to a diverse clientele including Government Institutions, Parastatals, Manufacturing Industries, Mining Companies, NGOs, and Private sector corporations.
